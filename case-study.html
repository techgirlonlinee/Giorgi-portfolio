Campfire

Description

Virtual campfire application to create a safe and exclusive community for youths after their rehabilitation period

Role

UX / UI Designer at Leavingstone

Client

Yeswecan Clinics

Problem

Yes We can Clinic (YWCC) treat youths with mental health issues, addictions, and behavioural problems. However, many of the youths (13-25 year olds) often fall back into the same cycle after the treatment because the lack of social support.

concept

A virtual campfire app as a method of intervention, providing an exclusive environment for Fellows and reduce the chances of relapse for youths. The app enables fellows to share daily “live” moments to stay connected with each other, even after the clinic. Being by the campfire, the fellows will be in each other’s warmth and share deep connections & similar experiences.

Framework

Campfire Session

Through the brief, we understood that the essential part of the project was actual session and the main requirement for the fellows to feel they’re part of fellow community again - we used the same gestures (open hand & fist) as they use throughout their physical sessions - So this gave us clue that two main buttons must be easily accessible and has to stand out, grab enough attention to compete with illustration scene and work towards united experience.

Art Direction

type

Tiempos Fine

The type aligns with the clinic’s branding which creates familiarity for the youth fellows to easily associate the app with the clinic itself. It is a modern serif that is contemporary, robust, and clear. It also has oscillating characteristics between playful yet warm curves and sharp details that youths who has been through a difficult time can relate to.

Open Sans

The utilitarian typeface provides a lot of legibility without grabbing too much attention, allowing the illustrations to shine. The humanist sans serif typeface provides a sense of friendliness and warm to ease the user through the app.

color & Shapes

Inspired by the campfire, two contrasting colours were selected: night blue for the UI background and elements, and orange to represent sunset for the accent colour. The colour combination creates a visually balanced feeling of a warmly lit campfire to comfort the user in the dark night.

Playful and nongeometric shapes helped us to create more joyful experience - they’re mostly inspired by the dinamic shape of campfire itself. The link between UI and the session scene is strong which emphesizes feeling of consistency.

illustrations

Illustrations are the essence of the application but with limited time this process of art direction was done simultaneously with UI design. We had to create delightful, cozy, and emotional experience that would please both client and customers while they have contrasting preferences, resulting in several iterations. The client preferred pixelated illustrations with darker colors while the customers liked bright colors with charcoal textured illustrations.

For the final results, we used storytelling to immerse users into the experience. We separated scene in to two parts: 1) the horizon and 2) the campfire. For the first scene we used sunset tones; for the second, we used darker colors but illuminated with campfire’s cozy light. With the help of scenes division, were used both palletes the sceen to satisfy both users and client.
